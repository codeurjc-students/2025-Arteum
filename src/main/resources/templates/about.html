Descubra nuestra esencia

ARTEUM es un trabajo de acción artística dedicada a reinventar la forma en que el arte se experimenta y se comparte. Combinando el compromiso con la comunidad, la innovación creativa y el diálogo cultural, apoyamos y promovemos obras de arte que trascienden fronteras, desde obras maestras clásicas hasta atrevidas expresiones contemporáneas. En ARTEUM creemos en el arte como catalizador de la reflexión, la conexión y el cambio.

Nuestro esfuerzo artístico

En Arteum, nuestra misión es revolucionar la experiencia del arte. Nos comprometemos a:

  • Empoderar a los artistas
  • Conectar gente de todo el mundo
  • Fomentar la diversidad
  • Garantizar la integridad
  • Construir una comunidad

Creemos que el arte tiene el poder de inspirar, transformar y conectar a las personas. Nuestro objetivo es dar vida a este poder.

Nuestra historia

A group of passionate art collectors and tech innovators, the company set out to make art auctions more accessible, transparent, and global.

The Vision Takes Shape

The first online auction was launched in early 2011, featuring a curated collection of contemporary artworks. This initial auction attracted bidders from across the globe, proving that there was a significant demand for a platform that allowed people to participate in art auctions from the comfort of their homes.

2011
Expanding the Horizon

By 2013, had expanded its offerings to include a diverse range of art genres, including modern, classical, and fine art. This growth was driven by a commitment to curating high-quality collections that appealed to both seasoned collectors and new art enthusiasts.

2013
Supporting Emerging Artists

In 2021, the platform launched an initiative to support emerging artists, offering them a dedicated space to showcase their work to a global audience. This move not only helped new artists gain recognition but also enriched the diversity of the auctions.

2021
Global Reach

By 2017, the platform had established itself as a leader in the online art auction industry, attracting an international audience of buyers and sellers. The year also saw the launch of multi-language support and global shipping options, further solidifying the company’s global presence.

2017
Continuing the Legacy

As of 2023, remains committed to its founding vision while continuing to innovate and adapt to the ever-changing art market. With thousands of successful auctions and a vibrant community of art lovers, the company looks forward to furthering its mission of making art accessible to everyone, everywhere.
